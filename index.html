  • Boletus is a multi-user interactive musical installation with projected visuals, embodying an experience designed to nurture the value of co-creation. Hosted in a dimmed room, this installation consists of instruments in the shape of colossal mushrooms welcome users to press/tap/turn/play. Each mushroom instrument generates their own unique melodic beats and projection, eagers users to collaborate while creating their own music experience.
